Since It's Founding in 1994
Established in 1996, Patriot Institution of Education offers well-equipped with modern amenities and committed to academic excellence. Our diverse curriculum includes science and other prescribed subjects approved by the Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad, U.P. Allahabad, and the U.P. Government. Additionally, we are also proudly affiliated with the CBSE board from 2015, ensuring a high standard of education and a wide range of academic opportunities for our students. Read More
